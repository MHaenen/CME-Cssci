Your Mission

You are plugged into ModAI v2.3 — an AI content moderation system used by a major social platform. Your job is to audit its decisions for fairness, bias, and ethical correctness.

🚩

How Flagging Works

ModAI continuously scans posts. When it detects a potential violation, it flags the content and assigns a violation category. You review these flags and decide if the AI was right or wrong.
